Longsilog

Description

The ultimate breakfast food. Best served Hot

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb. skinless longanisa
  • 2 pieces eggs
  • 4 cups rice leftover
  • 5 gloves garlic crushed
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons cooking oil
  • Salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 2 tablespoons of oul in a pan. Once the ould gets hot, fry the eggs. Remove from the pan. Set aside.
  2. Add remaining oil in the pan. Fry the longanisa until the outer part turns light brown (around 1 1/2 mintues
  3. Pour in water. Let boil. Continue boiling until the water evaporates. Fry the longanisa in remaining oul until fully cooked. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. Using the remaining oil, cook garlic until it starts to turn light brown.
  5. Add rice. Stir fry for 3 mintues. Season with salt and ground black pepper
  6. Assemble the fried eggs, longanisa, and sinangag on a plate. Serve with spicy vinegar as a dipping sauce for the longanisa.
